About Slow Living

"We are a magazine for people who want their lives to feel made by hand."

Slow Living is a quarterly print magazine and a small, calm website, made in Bergen by a team of five. We pay our writers properly, do not run sponsored content, and post one piece a week — never on weekends, never before the writer is ready.

If a piece is not finished, we hold it until it is. There is no schedule worth a thin essay.

The Sunday letter.

One short note every Sunday evening: what we have made this week, one paragraph we cannot stop thinking about, and a single small recipe.
