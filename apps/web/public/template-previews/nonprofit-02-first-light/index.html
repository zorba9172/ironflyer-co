What we do

Three programs, one goal: every kid reading on grade level.

Books at home

Bookshelf Build

Twelve grade-appropriate books delivered home to every K–3 student in a partner school — half in the child's home language. Families keep them forever.

Cost: $32 / child / year

In school

One-to-One Tutors

Three thirty-minute tutoring sessions a week with a trained, background-checked volunteer. The 12-week cohort moves the average student up 1.4 reading levels.

Cost: $148 / child / cohort

At home

Family Literacy Nights

Monthly evenings where families read together with a teacher facilitator, learn the same routines we use in tutoring, and take home a new book per child.

Cost: $640 / school / month
