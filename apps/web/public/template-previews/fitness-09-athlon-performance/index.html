The lab

Testing that actually changes the plan.

Every test produces a written, athlete-readable report and a coach hand-off. Numbers without a follow-up plan are noise.

/ 01 · Aerobic

VO₂ max & lactate

Cosmed K5 metabolic cart, treadmill or bike. Step-tested lactate to 6mmol. Full zone re-write delivered same day.

$320 · 90 min slot
/ 02 · Force

Force plate · CMJ + isometric

Counter-movement jump, isometric mid-thigh pull, single-leg drop landings. Tracked quarterly.

$210 · 60 min slot
/ 03 · Motion

Run gait capture

Twelve-camera Vicon system. Joint angles, cadence, ground contact, vertical oscillation. PDF report and 30-min coach walk-through.

$420 · 75 min slot
/ 04 · Body comp

DEXA scan

Quarterly body comp, regional lean mass, and bone density. Same-day report. Mostly used to make sure athletes aren't under-fueling.

$140 · 20 min slot
Services

Coaching, strength, return-to-sport.

/ Service 01

Performance coaching

Six-week blocks with quarterly retesting. Built around the events you care about.

  • Daily plan in TrainingPeaks
  • Weekly call · 30 min
  • Quarterly testing included
/ Service 02

Sport-specific strength

2-3 strength sessions a week, programmed off your force plate profile.

  • In-person or remote
  • Block reviews every 6 weeks
  • Velocity-based loading
/ Service 03

Return to sport

Post-injury bridge. Built with a sports PT and re-tested through the standard battery.

  • Single-leg force testing
  • Asymmetry tracking
  • Coordinated with your PT
/ Service 04

Team performance

Annual testing battery for college and pro teams. Aggregate reports for coaches, individual reports for athletes.

  • On-site days available
  • Force plate + 30-15
  • Roster-level dashboards
/ Service 05

Nutrition consulting

Sports RD on staff. Best paired with DEXA and a hard training block.

  • Two consults included
  • Fueling plan for race week
  • Body comp review
/ Service 06

Master plan

One-time consult for athletes self-coaching. We write the season; you execute it.

  • Annual periodization plan
  • Two re-tests per year
  • Email check-ins quarterly
Process

How we start with you.

Free intake

30-min call. We map your year, the events, and the windows.

Baseline battery

VO₂, force plate, motion capture. Half-day at the lab.

Plan delivered

Within seven days. Reviewed live, then loaded into TrainingPeaks.

Quarterly retest

Every 12 weeks. Plan adjusted in writing within five days.
