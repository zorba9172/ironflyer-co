How it works

Fair launches in three signed steps.

Connect, commit, claim. No insider rounds, no allowlist for friends-of-friends. Every wallet gets the same terms.

1

Connect & verify

Connect your wallet, complete optional KYC for allocation tiers over $5,000. Anti-bot signature challenge takes 6 seconds.

2

Commit your allocation

Deposit USDC into the sale contract. Allocation cap is enforced per wallet on-chain. Refunds are automatic if the sale undersubscribes.

3

Claim on TGE

At Token Generation Event, claim your initial unlock. The rest vests linearly per the project's published schedule, claimable any time.

Vesting is public, on-chain, enforced.

Every launch publishes its allocation breakdown and vesting schedule before the sale opens. Vesting contracts are deployed before TGE, and unlocks are visible on Etherscan. There are no off-chain pinky promises.

For builders

Run a launch without the regret.

We turn launch checklists into contracts. You bring the project; we bring the lockups, anti-bot, allocation, and vesting infrastructure that's been audited a dozen times.

⚐

Anti-bot signatures

EIP-712 challenge-response, IP heuristics, and on-chain rate limits. No private mempool advantage, no MEV sandwich during sale.

⛬

Allocation tiers

Capped per-wallet allocation with optional KYC tiers. Excess deposits are refunded automatically, no support tickets.

↻

Vesting contracts

OpenZeppelin VestingWallet with cliff, linear, and milestone schedules. Public timeline, queryable on Etherscan.

⌗

Liquidity bootstrap

Optional Balancer LBP or Uniswap V4 pool deployment with locked LP tokens. Listing happens via the launchpad contract, not a Discord post.

$

Treasury delivery

Raised funds are released to your treasury multisig only after KYC and vesting setup complete. We never custody your raise.

⛨

Independent review

Two internal reviewers and one external auditor must sign off before your launch lists. Review report is published with the sale page.
