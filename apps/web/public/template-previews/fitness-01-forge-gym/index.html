Programs

Pick the track that fits.

Three coached programs and an open floor. Every track is written by a coach, reviewed every six weeks, and adjusted to what you're actually recovering from.

/ 01 · Powerlifting

Forge Strength

Squat, bench, deadlift, accessories. Twelve-week blocks with a mock meet at the end. For lifters who want a number on the bar.

4× / week$220 / mo
/ 02 · Olympic

Snatch & Clean

Coached weightlifting, capped at six per session. Videoed lifts, position work, and a real warmup. USAW certified coaches only.

3× / week$260 / mo
/ 03 · General

Forge Foundations

For your first six months under a barbell. Mobility, technique, and a slow ramp to weight that actually challenges you.

2× / week$180 / mo
