// why route

The shortest path between intent and a URL.

No console clicking. No half-true YAML. RouteCloud reads your sentence, plans the resources, prices them, applies them, and hands back a URL you can curl.

01 · plan

Plans you can read

Every sentence renders to a structured plan: services, networks, IAM, regions. Reviewed before apply. Diffed on every redeploy.

02 · price

Cost before apply

RouteCloud quotes the monthly bill from your cloud's actual pricing table before it provisions a single resource. No surprises.

03 · apply

Real Terraform underneath

The plan compiles to versioned Terraform. You own the state. You can break glass and read the HCL whenever you want.

04 · run

Multi-cloud routing

AWS, GCP, Fly, Cloudflare. Pick the cheapest region that meets your latency budget. RouteCloud schedules; you keep the keys.

05 · roll

Rollback by sentence

route rollback orbit-api --to "1m ago". Every deploy is a tagged state. Reverting is a sentence, not a war story.

06 · own

You keep the keys

Resources land in your cloud account. Your domain, your registry, your IAM. We are a planner, not a landlord.
