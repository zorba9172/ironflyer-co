Groups

Three paces. No drop.

Every run splits into three groups, each with a designated leader. The leader runs the slowest pace, not the fastest. We finish together.

/ 8:30+ min/mi

What to bring

Showing up for the first time.

Shoes that fit

Anything you've been running in. We're not a sponsored shoe club.

A watch or your phone

You don't need a Garmin. Most members track on their phones.

Water for trail Sundays

The road runs have water stops. The trail run doesn't.

Layers in the winter

Portland winter means a base, a vest, gloves, and a hat. We meet rain or shine.

A reflective something

For evening runs. We have spares at the meetup.

$0

The club is free. The coffee after is on whoever PR'd that morning.
