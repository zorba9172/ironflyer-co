Where it goes

Real labs. Real grants. No middlemen.

A small selection of the 2025 grant cohort. Full grantee list and proposals are public on our research portal.

2025 grant cohort

Vasquez Lab · Penn

Investigating ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) progression markers using single-cell sequencing. A pilot grant that could change who gets watchful waiting and who gets surgery.

2-year pilot$420,000
2025 grant cohort

Adeyemo Lab · Johns Hopkins

Disparity research on dense-breast screening protocols for Black women under 40, where current guidelines fail catastrophically. Hope Runs is the lead funder.

3-year R-grant$680,000
2025 grant cohort

Chen Lab · Stanford

Triple-negative tumor microenvironment mapping. The lab's first preprint on lymphocyte distribution dropped in February. Replication is in progress.

2-year pilot$350,000
2025 grant cohort

Whitley Lab · UCSF

Long-term survivorship study: bone density, fatigue, and cognitive function 10+ years post-treatment. The cohort is at year 7.

10-year cohort$1.1M total
