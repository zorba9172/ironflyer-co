Designed in

Frames, not fashion accessories.

Highline began as one optometrist and one frame-maker who were tired of the same five shapes from the same conglomerate. Today, every Highline frame is made in a small acetate workshop in northern Italy, then prescription-cut and edged in Brooklyn.

We measure fit in millimeters, not vibes. We test lenses in glare. We replace frames that warp inside two years, no questions asked.
