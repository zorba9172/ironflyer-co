The farm

Two and a half acres in the valley.

We started with one small bed of zinnias in 2019. Today we grow on two and a half acres, no plastics, no aerial spray, with three full-season growers and a rotating apprentice.

Every stem you receive was alive that morning. If the weather is wrong for one variety, we tell you, and we substitute with something better, not something that arrived on a plane.
