Four cinemas in inner Portland

Where you watch.

Hollywood Theatre

4122 NE Sandy Blvd

The flagship venue. Restored 1926 single-screen with the largest seat count of the festival. Hosts the opening film, awards night, and most premieres.

384 seats35mm + DCPBar + cafe

Cinema 21

616 NW 21st Ave

The festival's second house. A 1926 single-screen on the west side, repaired and run by the same family since 1981.

290 seatsDCP onlyWine bar

341 SW 10th Ave

Six small screens downtown. Hosts our shorts blocks and the morning industry screenings.

A 1915 neighborhood cinema. The Saturday Night Special is always here, with one 35mm restoration each year.

Submissions for 2027

Submit a film.

Three submission windows for Cinema Fest IX (October 2027). Fee waivers available for filmmakers from low-income countries and for emerging filmmakers under 25.

One platform, three windows.

We submit through FilmFreeway. Every submission is watched in full by at least two programmers. No same-day decisions; expect a reply six to eight weeks after the close of your window.

We invite resubmissions. If you have screened with us before and your new film is ready, mention it in the cover letter and we will route it appropriately.
