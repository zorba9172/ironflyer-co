How it works

One real teacher, unlimited practice.

1

Diagnostic in 20 minutes

A 50-minute live video session with the same tutor, every week. You see the plan ahead of time and the notes after.

Mathwave's AI tutor coaches your child through 15 minutes of practice a day. It picks up exactly where the live lesson ended.

FAQ

Parents ask.

How old does my child need to be?

We tutor first grade through twelfth grade. Younger students typically work in 30-minute sessions; older students in 50.

Do you align with my school's curriculum?

Yes. After the diagnostic, you tell us your school and curriculum (e.g. Eureka, Singapore, IB). We adjust the plan so we are reinforcing, not contradicting.

What if my child doesn't click with their tutor?

You can request a new tutor any time, no questions asked. We rematch within 24 hours.

Is the AI practice really useful?

It is the difference. Twenty minutes of daily, adapted practice between live sessions multiplies the impact of the live lesson. The AI never replaces the teacher; it supports them.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscriptions cancel from the dashboard with one click. No phone calls, no retention dance.
