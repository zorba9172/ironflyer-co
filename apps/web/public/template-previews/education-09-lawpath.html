Method

Four elements.

Every track contains the same four elements in different proportions. They are the things that pass bar exams.

i.

Issue spotting

Twenty minutes a day spotting issues in real essays from the last decade. The skill the bar is testing.

ii.

Black letter

Concise outlines for each subject, refreshed each year against the released exam questions.

iii.

Writing under timer

You write at least one timed essay every day. Faculty grade two of them each week with comments.

iv.

MBE practice

Daily question sets with rationale review. Performance tracked subject by subject, week by week.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is the pass guarantee?

If you complete the UBE Full program — every lecture, every graded essay, both mock exams — and do not pass on your first attempt, we refund the tuition or extend you a free retake program.

Do you cover non-UBE jurisdictions?

We cover all 41 UBE jurisdictions. For California and Florida, we offer supplemental state-specific modules at additional cost.

Are the materials in print or digital?

Both. The full set of printed outlines and a hardcover review book ships with the UBE Full and Retake programs at no extra charge.

Who grades the essays?

Practicing attorneys with bar admission in a UBE jurisdiction. Each essay returns within seven days with margin comments and a section-by-section score.

Can I switch from another bar prep program?

Yes. We honor a partial credit if you can show a paid receipt from a competing program for the same exam administration. Contact admissions for details.
