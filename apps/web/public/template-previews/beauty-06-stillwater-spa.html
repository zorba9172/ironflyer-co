Three things we believe

A spa is a place that doesn't rush you.

01

One arrival, one room

Every appointment includes thirty minutes in the salt grotto before your treatment. You don't shuttle between rooms — your therapist comes to you.

02

Pressure, not perfume

Our oils are unscented unless you ask. The work matters more than the marketing. Our therapists are bodyworkers, not aromatherapy salespeople.

03

The phone goes in the locker

We're a quiet space by design. No music in the hallway, no overhead announcements, no notifications. You'll find your phone where you left it.

The space

Eight rooms, two gardens, one quiet hallway.

The Sage Room

Our largest treatment room, with garden access. Best for couples massage and long-form sessions.

The Salt Grotto

A private room walled in pink salt brick with a heated chaise. Forty-five minutes, by yourself, with a glass of cold water.

The Facial Room

A dedicated facial suite with a magnifying lamp, hydraulic chair, and our own product line in clear bottles by the sink.
