How it works

Three nights, every week.

01

Monday: the lecture

A 90-minute lecture from someone who has run the thing, not someone who has written about it. Recorded.

02

Wednesday: office hours

Two operators take questions for two hours. Bring your problem, leave with a plan. Not optional.

03

Friday: review

Every founder shows the week's deliverable to the cohort. Five minutes. We tell each other the truth.

FAQ

Questions before you apply.

Do I need to be building already?

Yes. You should have either a working prototype or at least 5 customer interviews in the bag. Founder Track is not where you decide what to build; it is where you ship what you are already building.

Is it remote, in-person, or hybrid?

Remote-first with a 3-day in-person retreat at the start of week six in Lisbon. Demo day is hybrid: founders pitch in NYC, investors join in person or live.

Do you take equity?

No. Founder Track is a tuition-based program. We do not take any equity, board seat, or future right of investment from cohort companies.

How competitive is admission?

We accept about 22 founders per cohort from roughly 350 applications. Decision is based on velocity in the last six months, not credentials.

What is the refund policy?

Withdraw before the end of week two for a full refund. After week two, refunds are pro-rated for weeks not started.
