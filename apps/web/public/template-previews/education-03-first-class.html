FAQ

Before you book.

How are teachers vetted?

Every teacher submits credentials, a video sample, and a teaching plan. We run a 30-minute live interview. Less than 18% of applicants are accepted.

What happens if my teacher cancels?

You get an automatic refund and credit for the lesson. We surface backup teachers who match your level and schedule within a few minutes.

Can I switch teachers later?

Yes. Many students work with two teachers — one for grammar, one for conversation. Lesson packs are tied to a single teacher; pay-as-you-go is not.

Do you provide lesson materials?

Teachers bring their own. Most use a shared Notion or Google Doc per student. We supply a built-in whiteboard, video, and lesson note system.

How do payouts work for teachers?

Teachers keep 85% of their hourly rate. Payouts go out every Friday via Stripe Connect to 32 supported countries.
