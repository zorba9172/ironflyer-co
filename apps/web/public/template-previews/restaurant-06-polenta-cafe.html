Hello

We're Sarah and Tom.

We opened Polenta in 2019 above a hardware shop on Linden Lane. Sarah ran the bakery at a vineyard in Sonoma. Tom was a barista in Melbourne for ten years and a coffee buyer for five.

We wanted a café that felt like a friend's kitchen — a place to read for an hour, eat a slice of cake, walk home with a loaf of bread. Quiet music, soft chairs, good cups.

We bake the bread, pull the shots, write the chalkboard. If you have a question, ask us. We are usually behind the counter.
