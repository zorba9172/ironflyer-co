HOW_IT_WORKS

Five steps, sealed before ground breaks.

We do not change-order our buyers. The price you sign at contract is the price you pay at closing. The timeline is the same.

01 / DESIGN

Architect-sealed plan

You select from three sealed plans per neighborhood. No bespoke redesign mid-build.

Week 0
02 / CONTRACT

Fixed price, fixed date

Final price and target delivery date are signed into the purchase agreement.

Week 2
03 / BUILD

In-house crews

Foundation through trim handled by Modern Plot crews on Modern Plot equipment.

Weeks 4–36
04 / FINISH

Selections, on schedule

Two finish meetings on a fixed calendar — cabinetry, tile, and lighting only.

Weeks 28, 32
05 / DELIVERY

Walk-through + handover

Pre-closing punch list cleared on a single day. Keys handed over on the contracted date.

SPECIFICATION

What every Modern Plot home actually has.

The base specification is published. No upgrade packages, no surprise downgrades, no allowances under-funded at contract.

Base specification, all homes

Every Modern Plot home ships with this minimum spec. Plans differ in floor area and program, not in build quality.

FOUNDATION
Reinforced poured concrete, 12" footings
FRAMING
FSC-certified engineered lumber, no MDF structure
ENVELOPE
R-30 walls, R-50 roof, ZIP system sheathing
WINDOWS
Triple-pane aluminum-clad timber, U-factor 0.18
HVAC
Heat-pump primary, ERV ventilation, no gas appliances
ELECTRICAL
200A panel, dedicated EV circuit, solar-ready conduit
FINISH
White oak floors, quartz counters, integrated appliances
WARRANTY
10-year structural, 2-year mechanical, lifetime envelope
