[06] FAQ

The practical details.

Are talks recorded?

Yes. Posted to the public archive within 30 days of the conference. Attendees get a private link the day after.

Will there be a panel?

No. We do not run panels. We have tried; they always become the worst session of the day.

Refund policy?

Full refund through August 14. After that, tickets are transferable but not refundable.

Scholarships?

40 scholarships available to underrepresented engineers. Application open July 1 through August 14.

Code of conduct?

Published, enforced, with a named ombudsperson on-site both days. Read it before you register.

Dietary needs?

Catering covers vegan, vegetarian, halal, and gluten-free by default. Note allergies on registration and we will route around them.
