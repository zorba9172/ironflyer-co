A desert,
and a place to lie down in it.

Forty rooms cut into a sandstone ledge above the Tzin riverbed. A spring-fed mineral pool, a date-grove kitchen, and a sky observatory that opens every clear night.

Experiences

Quiet things to do in a wide place.

Getting here

Two hours south of everything.

Tel Aviv to Bloom is a two-hour drive. We send a car if you call ahead. There is no village within twenty kilometres. The road in is one paved lane, and you will see the cliffs from a distance.
