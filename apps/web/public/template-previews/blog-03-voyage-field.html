About the journal

We pay our own way, and we stay until the place stops performing.

Voyage Field is a reader-supported quarterly. We do not accept sponsored travel, comp'd hotels, or tourism-board commissions. Every writer travels on the journal's commission, stays at least three weeks, and is expected to come back with a single, slow piece of writing and the photographs that go with it.

The result is fewer destinations and longer essays. We are fine with that.

The Sunday letter

One dispatch, every other Sunday.

A short letter twice a month: where our writers are, one paragraph we cannot stop thinking about, and a single photograph.
