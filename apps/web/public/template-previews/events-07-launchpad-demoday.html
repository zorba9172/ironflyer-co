[07] FAQ

The practical stuff.

Do you take equity?

No. Launchpad is free for founders, ever, forever. We are underwritten by sponsors and a small ticketed press section.

Who can attend?

Investors with a deployed fund, and operators by referral. We do not sell tickets to the public.

Is it streamed?

Yes, to a private feed for operators who can't attend. Recordings are deleted after 30 days.

Why no AI cohort?

We have an AI cohort whenever the best applications happen to be AI. We do not have a quota.

Do international founders apply?

Yes. 41% of cohort 013 was outside the US. We help with travel.

What about press?

One reporter per outlet, by application. No livestreaming, no recording inside the hall.

Can my company sponsor?

We take three sponsors per cycle, capped. We turn down sponsorships from funds and from anyone selling to early-stage founders. Email sponsor@launchpad.demo for the deck.

Can I attend if I'm just curious?

No. The room is sized for the cohort plus 240 confirmed-active investors. We protect the founders' time, not the audience number.
