Our mission

Three tools. One coalition.

We don't post slogans. We file briefs, draft bills, and train organizers. The three pillars below are how we work in every state where we're active.

PILLAR ONE

Impact litigation

Voting rights, criminal-legal reform, reproductive freedom, immigration. 38 active cases in 17 state and federal courts. Public docket, public briefs.

PILLAR TWO

State & federal policy

Bill-drafting partnerships with 22 statehouse caucuses. 14 bills introduced in 2025, four signed. Plain-language legislative tracker is public.

PILLAR THREE

Organizer training

Four-month paid fellowship in community organizing, base-building, and rapid-response media. 184 fellows graduated since 2018.

From the docket

Four active cases.

A small selection from the 38-case docket. Briefs, filings, and oral-argument recordings are public on our case portal.

Voting rights · 5th Circuit

Garrett v. Texas

Challenge to 2024 voter-roll purge that removed 184,000 registered voters in Harris County without notice. Emergency stay filed October 2025.

In litigationFiled Jan 2025Lead counsel: M. Reyes
Criminal-legal · 9th Circuit

Sanford v. Cooper

Class-action against a county jail's use of solitary confinement on adolescents under 18. Cert. granted October 2025. Settlement framework on the table.

SettlementFiled Aug 2023Lead counsel: J. Park
Repro. freedom · State court · OH

In re Marisa H.

Equal-protection challenge to an Ohio statute imposing parental-consent rules that conflict with the state constitutional amendment passed in 2023.

BriefingFiled May 2025Lead counsel: A. Whitfield
Immigration · D.D.C.

Castillo et al. v. DHS

Due-process challenge to ICE detention of asylum-seekers without 48-hour hearings. Filed jointly with three regional immigration legal aids.

DiscoveryFiled Mar 2025Lead counsel: H. Mensah
