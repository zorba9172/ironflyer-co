The method

Diagnose first. Treat slowly. Re-measure.

We don't sell treatments à la carte. Every client starts with a one-hour consult, a SkinScan AI imaging analysis, and a written plan with cost and timeline before we book anything.

01

The consult

One hour. We look at your skin under magnification, review your history and meds, and shoot SkinScan images of pigment, texture, and redness.

02

The plan

A written, costed plan with timeline. Most plans run three to six months. We won't book treatments without it.

03

The treatments

We start gentle, re-measure with SkinScan every two months, and adjust the plan based on what your skin is actually doing.

04

The maintenance

Once you're where you want to be, most clients move to a monthly Atelier facial and a stable home routine.
