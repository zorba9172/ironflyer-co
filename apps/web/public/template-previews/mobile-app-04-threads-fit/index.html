Why Threads

One log. Both engines.

Most apps make you pick: a strength logger or a run tracker. Threads is one weekly plan that schedules both and adapts when something goes sideways.

⤷

Hybrid plans

5-3-1, lyle, push-pull-legs, and a few originals — all paired with zone-2 days and a weekly interval session.

⌬

Smart deloads

Miss a session, sleep badly, RPE creeps up? Threads slides the week and drops volume without you doing math.

♥

Heart-rate aware

Pairs with Apple Watch, Garmin, Polar, and Wahoo. Caps zone-2 by HR, not pace. No more accidentally racing easy days.

→

Fast set entry

Two taps to log a set. Auto-fills weight + reps from last week. PR detection without nagging.

↗

Charts that matter

Estimated 1RM, zone-2 efficiency, weekly tonnage, and a sleep / RPE overlay so trends mean something.

∞

Export everything

CSV, TCX, FIT, and a public API. Your training history is yours. Move it anywhere.

Get going

Programmed in three minutes.

01

Pick a plan

Tell Threads your lift max, your easy pace, and how many days a week you can train. Pick from eight templates.

Sets, runs, intervals — log everything in under thirty seconds. Threads pulls heart rate from your watch.

FAQ

Reasonable questions.

What does Threads cost?

Free for three plan weeks. After that, $7 a month or $59 a year. No ads, no tier-gated charts.

Which watches does it pair with?

Apple Watch (Series 5+), Garmin (FR-class and up), Polar H10, Wahoo Tickr, and any standard BLE chest strap.

Can I use Threads if I only lift?

Yes. Just don't add a run day in setup — Threads will give you a pure strength plan and the conditioning slot stays empty.

Where do my workouts live?

On your device, with optional encrypted cloud sync. Full CSV / FIT export from settings. No vendor lock.

Is there a web dashboard?

Yes — threads.app/dashboard. Read-only for now, full edit in v3.
