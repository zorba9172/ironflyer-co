Why Stillwater

The opposite of a wellness funnel.

No upsell screens, no fake science, no influencer chasing you to a course. A small library of real practices, recorded carefully, kept on your phone.

○

Real teachers

Eight teachers, eight traditions. Anapana, metta, body scan, classical breathwork, somatic check-in. Their bios link to the work that trained them.

⌖

Quiet sessions

Twenty minutes of well-made silence — room tone, soft cues, no music bed. The best feature in the app, the one you grow into.

~

Breath pacers

Box, 4-7-8, coherent, and physiological-sigh pacers with visual guides. Save your favorite as a home-screen widget.

∮

One-line journal

After each session, one prompt: "What stayed?" Type or speak a sentence. Stored privately, scannable in calendar view.

⏚

Offline-first

All sessions cache on first play. Stillwater works on the train, on a plane, on a cabin weekend without service.

⛬

Subtle reminders

One reminder, at the time you actually meditate. No "you've been gone for 3 days, come back" guilt nags. Ever.

FAQ

Gentle questions.

How much does Stillwater cost?

The first 20 sessions and all breath pacers are free, forever. Stillwater Plus is $5 a month or $42 a year and unlocks the whole library, including the silence shelf and the offline cache.

Who teaches?

Eight teachers from eight lineages. Each bio links to the work that trained them and the school they teach with outside the app.

Is this a religious app?

No. The practices come from contemplative traditions, taught in their secular form. Where a session draws from a specific lineage, the teacher names it openly.

Will it nag me?

One optional reminder a day, at the time you choose. No streak guilt, no "you've been gone" emails.

Where does my data live?

The journal entries are on your device. The optional cloud backup is end-to-end encrypted. We have no analytics SDK and no advertising ID access.

Sit a little. That's all.

The first twenty sessions are free. The shelf is small on purpose. Take what you need.
