Questions

What clients actually ask.

Is the consult free?

The first consult is $145, applied to your treatment if you book within 30 days. We don't do free consults — they reward upsell, not advice.

Will it look like I had work done?

That's the wrong outcome and we don't deliver it. Dr. Han under-dials by default. We'd rather see you back in two weeks for a tiny top-up than overshoot once.

How long until I see results?

Botox: 7–14 days. Filler: immediate, settles at two weeks. Sculptra: three months. We'll write the timeline on your plan.

What about downtime and bruising?

Most injectable appointments have 1–3 days of mild bruising and swelling. Morpheus and TCA peels have visible downtime of 3–7 days. We'll be specific.

Do you treat clients in their twenties?

Sometimes — preventative micro-doses or under-eye work, where appropriate. We're equally happy to send you home with a skincare plan instead.
