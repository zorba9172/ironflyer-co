What it does

Everything for the roster. Nothing for the dashboard.

BeaconHR replaces three half-built tools with one that's actually finished. Designed for the people who say "good morning" to teammates by name.

✦

Onboarding that breathes

A real first week — laptop, accounts, mentor, lunch — orchestrated by week, not a Trello board. New hires arrive prepared, not paged.

☼

Time off that asks softly

One-tap PTO request. Auto-balanced accruals. Coverage suggestions. Approval Slack message your manager actually reads.

❀

Reviews humans write

Drafts pulled from real work — shipped projects, calls, kudos — so writing a review takes twenty minutes, not a Saturday.

⌗

People analytics, gently

Tenure, comp bands, attrition signals, DEI rollups — visible, exportable, and never on a screen new hires can see.

⎈

Compliance, quietly

I-9, EEO-1, work authorization, GDPR data export. We do the paperwork and the audit trail. You do the warm welcome.

✿

Payroll bridges

Native integrations with Gusto, Deel, Remote, ADP. One source of truth for hires, leaves, comp changes, and offboarding.

Our approach

People work doesn't belong in a ticket queue.

HR teams aren't IT support. They're the institutional memory of a company. BeaconHR is built on that idea — files that read like notebooks, profiles that feel like portraits, dashboards that fade when you're not using them.
