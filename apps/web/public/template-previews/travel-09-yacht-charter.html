How a charter works

Four steps,
from brief to lift-off.

01

You send a brief

Week, region, guest count, indicative budget. No more than five lines.

02

We build a shortlist

Within twenty-four hours, three to five yachts that fit the brief. Built by a broker, not an algorithm.

03

You meet the captains

One call per yacht with the captain, the chef, and our chief broker on the line.

04

You step on board

MYBA contract, APA paid in, preference sheet sent to the chef. The boat is yours for the week.
