The fifth annual Quill conference · Edinburgh · September 8–10, 2026

A conference on retrieval, language, and human reasoning.

Three days of long-form papers and pointed discussion on the disciplines around modern language models: retrieval, evaluation, alignment, and the use of language to do real reasoning. Hosted by the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh.
