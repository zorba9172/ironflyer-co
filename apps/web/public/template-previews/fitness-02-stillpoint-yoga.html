A practice. Not a performance.

Stillpoint is a small studio teaching vinyasa, restorative, and yin. Twelve mats per class. Real silence between cues. Candles instead of speakers.

First class · $15 See the schedule
"Move slow enough to feel it."
01

Small rooms

Twelve mats. The teacher learns your name in the first class and remembers your shoulder by the second.

02

Quiet light

Beeswax candles and a single salt lamp. No tracking watches, no Insta wall, no playlist that loops in 28 minutes.

03

Long apprenticeship

Every teacher has at least 500 hours of training and three years on a mat before they hold a room of yours.

Classes

Four kinds of slowing down.

Every class is taught by a senior teacher. There are no recorded sessions, no app, no leaderboard.

All levels

Vinyasa

A warm, flowing class linking breath and movement. Music kept under your voice, never over it.
