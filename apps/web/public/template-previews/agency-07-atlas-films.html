// 01 — On the work

We’d rather make one good film than eight bad ones.

The brief comes in. We listen. We argue about it. We come back with a story, not a shot list. Then we make the shot list.

Most of the work that wins isn’t about the camera. It’s about whether the company has something honest to say and whether they’re brave enough to say it in 90 seconds without a voiceover.

If you’re here, you’ve probably watched the reel. We’d love to talk — bring a paragraph, not a deck.

Six formats we work in.

i.

Brand film

The 90-second piece that lives on the homepage and at the next sales meeting. Written, shot, scored, finished. From $120K.

Launch film

// 04 — How we work

From brief to master.

i.

Listen & write

A two-week paid scoping. We come back with a written treatment, not a pitch deck. If we’re not the right director, we say so.

ii.

Pre-production

Casting, locations, a director’s notebook with stills references, a shot list. You see all of it before we roll.

iii.

Production

One to three shoot days. We staff lightly — never the eighty-person crew. You’re welcome on set; most clients come at lunch.

iv.

Edit & finish

Two editorial passes, one colour, one sound. We deliver broadcast masters, social cuts, and an archive copy on hard drive in your name.
