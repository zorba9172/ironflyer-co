Manifesto · 01

We’re not interested in the launch. We’re interested in year three.

Most agencies are paid to make a first impression. We’re paid to make the thirtieth one. The brand that holds up when sales is on its fourth pitch deck. The product that still looks like itself after engineering has tripled. The site that doesn’t need rebuilding the next time strategy changes.

That means we move slower at the start. We ask harder questions about who you’re actually for. And we leave you with systems, not screenshots.

How we work

Four phases. About sixteen weeks.

Every engagement starts with a 2-week paid scoping sprint. After that, you decide whether we’re the right studio. Eighty percent of clients say yes.

PHASE 01

Listen & map

Interviews with your team, your customers, your investors. Competitive audit. We write what we heard back to you, in your words.

PHASE 02

Position

A short, signed positioning document. Who you’re for, who you’re not for, and what changes about how you talk about it.

PHASE 03

Design the system

Two rounds of identity, type, color, and a working application — usually a homepage and a pitch deck. We design in context, not in moodboards.

PHASE 04

Ship & document

Production files, brand site, written guidelines. We stay on a four-month retainer so the first real use of the brand isn’t the launch day surprise.

Studio

A studio of six. On purpose.

Northstand was founded in 2017 by two former in-house design leads who got tired of agencies handing off the strategy work to the cheapest available junior. We’ve grown to six people and we’re not interested in growing further.

Every client gets a partner on the engagement — no account manager between you and the designer. We take on roughly eight new clients a year, which is why we can take them seriously.
