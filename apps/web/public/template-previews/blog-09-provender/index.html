About Provender

"We write about food the way we would talk about food. Slowly, with our mouths half full."

Provender is a print magazine of food writing, made in Sevilla and Stockholm, paid for by readers. We do not run advertising. We do not accept comp'd meals or sponsored recipes. Every cook we write about is paid for their time, every photographer is paid for their pictures, and every recipe is tested at least four times by a real human in a real home kitchen.

If something we publish is wrong, we say so, in print, in the next issue.

— the editors
