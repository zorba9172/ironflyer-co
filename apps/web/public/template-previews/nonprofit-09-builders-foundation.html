programs.go

Three programs. One block at a time.

REHAB

House Rehab Program

We buy from land banks, rehab to code with our own crews, and sell at cost to qualifying working families. 30-year fixed mortgages at below-market rates through partner CDFIs.

$148,400 avg cost · 30yr fixed @ 4.5%

TRADES

Builders Trades Academy

A 24-month paid apprenticeship in carpentry, electric, plumbing, or HVAC. We hire from the neighborhoods we work in. 92% of graduates are employed in the trade at 18 months.

$22/hr paid + benefits · 24-month track

COMMUNITY

Block Stewardship

We never acquire scattered-site. We work block-by-block, with a community advisory committee that approves every acquisition. Existing residents stay put — no displacement.

42 blocks · 9 cities · 0 displacements
