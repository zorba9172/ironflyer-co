architecture

A zk-rollup, done properly.

Three layers: an EVM execution layer, a proving system, and an Ethereum settlement contract. Every state transition is proven; nothing is trusted.

01

Execute on L2

Transactions execute in our EVM-equivalent sequencer at 4,200 TPS. The same Solidity, the same bytecode, the same tooling — without re-deploying anything.

02

Prove the batch

Every 2 seconds, the prover generates a zk-SNARK over the batch. The proof is small (~50KB), fast to verify (~12ms), and posted to Ethereum within one slot.

03

Settle on L1

The Ethereum verifier checks the proof and finalizes the batch. From that block onward, withdrawals from L2 are honored at the source by the rollup contract.
