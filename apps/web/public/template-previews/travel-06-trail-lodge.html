A day at the lodge

FROM DAWN
TO DOWN.

The lodge runs on the rhythm of the mountain, not the clock on the wall. This is the cadence most days settle into.

  • 05:30

    Coffee on

    The kitchen is open. Oats, eggs, three kinds of jam, and the weather report from the ranger.

  • 07:00

    Walk out

    Guided parties leave together. Lunch in the pack, beta on the table by the door.

  • 14:30

    Back at the porch

    Boots off, beer or kombucha on the deck, sauna fired up by Reggie around three.

  • 18:30

    A drink in the hall

    Cocktails in the library, fire lit, a rough debrief of the day with whoever climbed what.

  • 19:00

    Dinner at the long table

    Four courses, one menu, twenty seats. Cooked by Marcus, who used to run a smokehouse in Bozeman.

  • 22:00

    Lights out

    The generator goes off. Stars do not.

Getting here

JACKSON HOLE,
THEN UP THE TRAIL.

Jackson Hole airport to the trailhead is two hours by car. From the trailhead it is a 3.2 mile walk in with a 1,400 foot gain. We send a mule team for your bag if you ask. Drop us a line a week ahead.
