The release process.

Three steps. Mint, render, archive.
i

The seed

When you mint, the contract records a 32-byte seed derived from the block hash and your address. That seed is the artwork's deterministic input forever.

ii

The render

The artwork code lives on IPFS and Arweave, referenced by hash on the contract. Any client (including a plotter) can re-render the piece from the seed.

iii

The royalty

Royalties are enforced via EIP-2981 and Chronos's allowlist. Artists receive 7.5% on every secondary transfer, paid directly from the marketplace contract.

Provenance on the ledger.

Every Chronos edition is an ERC-721 with an immutable seed, an IPFS-pinned render script, and an Arweave fallback. The contract is the canonical record of who minted what, when, and from which transaction.

We do not host any image you can't reconstruct independently. We do not move tokens you didn't sign. The platform fee is 2.5%, hard-coded, and visible on every receipt.
