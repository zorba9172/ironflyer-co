The Starter

Meet Lucille.

Every loaf and every croissant on the shelf owes itself to a small jar of bubbling flour-and-water named Lucille. We built her in the back of a one-bedroom apartment in 2016 from rye flour and the wild yeast living in the air above the Hudson river.

She is fed twice a day, every day. She has missed exactly two feedings — once because of a snowstorm, once because we forgot — and both times she sulked for a week.
