How it works

It is simpler than after-school.

1

Pick a class together

Show your child the six classes. Let them pick. Each class has a one-minute video of a kid showing what they made.

2

Join from home, 40 min/wk

Same day, same time, same six kids. The teacher leads on Zoom; your child needs supplies, a chair, and a snack.

3

Hang the result on the fridge

Every class ends with something real — a painting, a comic, a song, a film. The fridge will fill up.

FAQ

What parents ask.

How much screen time is involved?

The live session is 40 minutes on Zoom. Everything between sessions is hands-on with the supplies we ship — paper, paint, clay, instruments. No homework apps.

What if my child is shy?

The cap is six children. Most teachers run a five-minute "warm-up draw" at the start where every kid shows something they made that week. Shy kids settle in by week two.

What if we miss a week?

Every session is recorded and posted within 24 hours. Your child can watch and catch up. Teachers also offer one free make-up slot per term.

How do supplies arrive?

Supplies ship two weeks before class one and arrive in a single box. We replace anything missing or damaged for free.

Can I cancel mid-term?

Yes. Cancel any week from the parent dashboard. We refund the remaining weeks pro-rated, minus the cost of supplies shipped.
