A sample schedule · The Eastern Express

From Paris to Istanbul, in six nights.

An indicative timetable from our June 2026 departure. Day stops are walking tours, dinners ashore, or simply a long lunch in a station-front café.

Paris
Aperitif in the bar car, departure under the great arch of Gare de l'Est.
Day 1 · 18:54
Munich

The cabins

Guests

From the guest book.

Voyage Rail keeps a leather guest book in each dining car. These entries are reproduced with permission, lightly edited for length.

"I have travelled by train for sixty years and I had given up. This is the first time in twenty I have not flown to a wedding. We arrived rested. We arrived elsewhere."
Lord Henry M. · Paris → Istanbul · June 2025
"The violinist who plays the dining car is a Vienna Phil retiree. He played for our table at midnight on our anniversary. I will never forget it."
Annabelle & Arnaud V. · Lisbon → Vienna · May 2025
"Watching the Sea of Japan from a bed, with a bowl of nigiri on the lap-tray. Train travel as it should be — not nostalgic, alive."
Sara T. · Kyoto → Kobe · October 2025
"Mr Tran, our porter on the Reunification, remembered our cabin number, our breakfast, and the colour of my wife's robe by the morning of day two."
