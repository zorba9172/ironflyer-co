How we work

Four evidence-based approaches.

We don't believe in one method for everything. Your therapist will explain which approach fits and why, and adjust as you go.

CBT

Cognitive behavioral therapy

Structured, skills-based work for anxiety, OCD, and depression. Homework if it helps.

IFS

Internal family systems

A parts-based approach for trauma, perfectionism, and complicated inner critics.

EMDR

Eye-movement reprocessing

Targeted work for single-event trauma and persistent intrusive memories.

EFT

Emotionally focused therapy

For couples. Structured around attachment patterns and repairing the connection underneath the argument.

FAQ

The things people ask first.

Do you take insurance?

We're out-of-network but issue superbills. Many California PPO plans reimburse 50-80% of out-of-network mental health visits. We can help you call ahead and verify benefits.

How long does therapy usually last?

Many clients work with us for 4-12 months. Some stay longer for ongoing support. We check in at the 8-week mark and ask if we're moving in a useful direction.

Is online therapy as effective?

Research shows telehealth is as effective as in-person for most conditions. Most of our clients pick a hybrid mix. EMDR is the one modality we recommend in person when possible.

What if I don't click with my therapist?

That happens, and we'd rather know. Tell them or tell us. We'll set up a free 20-minute consult with another therapist on the team and waive the first session fee.

Can I bring my partner sometimes?

Yes. Many individual clients add 2-3 couples sessions during their work with us. It's billed at the couples rate.
