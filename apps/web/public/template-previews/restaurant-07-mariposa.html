Chef · Owner

Manuel Aguilar.

Manuel grew up in Oaxaca City and trained at Pujol in Mexico City under Chef Olvera. He moved to San Francisco in 2017 to work the line at Californios, and opened Mariposa in 2022 on a quiet stretch of Mission Street.

The kitchen is built around three obsessions: heirloom corn, live fire, and the bar. Every tortilla starts as dry corn that arrives by mail from a co-op in San Pedro Cajonos. The grill burns mesquite from Sonora. The bar lists mezcals you can't get anywhere else in the city.

The food is not "elevated." It is just cooked with care, from ingredients we sourced ourselves.
