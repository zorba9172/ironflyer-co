Our philosophy

A residence is never simply a transaction.

The Crown Group represents twenty-eight to thirty residences each year. Each principal is met in person. Each property is photographed in private. The book of available residences is shared, not advertised.

We have not run an open viewing since 1994. We have not paid for a billboard since the firm was founded. The catalog circulates through twelve cities by introduction, and that has been enough.

— Arthur Bellamy, Chairman
Advisory

Three quiet practices, by sole mandate.

The Crown Group works exclusively under sole mandate. We do not split commissions and we do not co-list. The principal hires us, and we represent the principal.

I.

Private sale representation

Trophy residence dispositions handled discreetly, with controlled introductions to a curated buyer register held confidentially within the firm.

II.

Acquisition advisory

Off-market sourcing across our twelve markets. Underwriting, negotiation, and closing oversight on behalf of principals and family offices.

III.

Portfolio review

An annual confidential valuation and disposition strategy across a principal's residential holdings, prepared in collaboration with counsel.
