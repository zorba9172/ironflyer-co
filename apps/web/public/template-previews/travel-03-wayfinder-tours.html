A sample itinerary · Patagonia 12 day

What twelve days actually looks like.

This is the day-by-day from our November 2026 Patagonia departure. Every itinerary on the site reads at this level of detail before you book.

November 2 — November 13 · 2026
01Sun

Land in Punta Arenas

Group dinner at Damiana's. Kit check. Your lead guide Constanza meets you at the hotel at 7.

02Mon

Punta Arenas → Torres del Paine

Bus north, lunch at a roadside parrilla, settle into the lodge inside the park by sundown.

03Tue

The Towers

The classic day hike to the base of the towers. 9 hours, 22 km. Empanadas at the top.

04Wed

Grey Glacier

Catamaran across Lago Grey, ice walk with Bernardo, and a long boat ride back to the dock.

05Thu

Rest day at the lodge

Sleep in. Optional horseback ride to Lago Pehoé. Asado dinner.

06Fri

Down to El Calafate

Cross the border by mid-morning. Afternoon free in town. Dinner at La Tablita.

07Sat

Perito Moreno glacier

Full day on the ice with the team from Hielo & Aventura. Wine and steaks at the lodge.

08Sun

To El Chaltén

Three-hour drive to the climbing village. Free afternoon, group dinner at La Tapera.

09Mon

Laguna de los Tres

The day hike to the base of Fitz Roy. 20 km, 8 hours.

10Tue

The estancia

Out to Inés and Roberto's estancia for a working day. Roast lamb. Stars.

11Wed

El Chaltén → Buenos Aires

Morning hike with optional refuelling, then flight to BA, last group dinner at Don Julio.

12Thu

Onward

Onward connections handled. We stay in town until your flight is wheels up.
