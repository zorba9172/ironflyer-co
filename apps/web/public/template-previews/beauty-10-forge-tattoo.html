// What we do

Five styles, six artists.

Every artist owns a style. We won't push you toward an artist whose work doesn't match what you actually want.

// 01

Fine line

Single-needle, delicate work. Botanicals, micro-script, small framework pieces.

// 02

Blackwork

Bold black, geometric, ornamental. Heavy contrast. Designed to age beautifully.

// 03

Traditional

American traditional, neo-trad. Bright color, thick line, will look like itself in twenty years.

// 04

Illustrative

Custom illustrative narratives, storybook tattoos, sketch-style portraits.

// 05

Cover-ups

Strategic transformation of older work. By consult only — bring photos of the existing piece.

// 06

Sleeves & back

Multi-session, long-form work. We'll build out a plan across three to ten sittings.

// 07

Lettering

Custom-drawn script, blackletter, sans serif. Never pulled from a font menu.

// 08

Memorial

Quiet, careful work for memorial pieces. Free consult, no upsell, no clock.

// Process

How a tattoo gets made here.

01

The brief

Send us your idea, reference images, placement, and rough size. We'll match you to the right artist.

02

The consult

30-minute in-person or video consult. We look at placement, talk size, and quote a total estimate.

03

The design

A $100 deposit holds your appointment and pays for the custom design, applied to your final total.

04

The session

The actual tattoo. Sterile single-use needles. We don't rush — most pieces take longer than the estimate suggests.
