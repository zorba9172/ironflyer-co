Method

The kitchen as a lab.

Algae does not "imitate" meat. We do not put cashew cream on something and pretend it's cheese. The kitchen is a fermentation lab — koji rice, lactic ferments, vinegar mothers, garums made from sunflower and pumpkin seed. We work with what plants can do that animals cannot.

Most of the flavor on the menu took weeks or months to build. A few took years.

The Farm · Plover Hill

One farm. Two hours away.

Plover Hill is a six-acre farm in Hudson, NY, run by Maya and David Linwood. They grow forty heirloom varieties — most of them not commercially viable — and ship a single van down to Brooklyn every Tuesday morning. The menu is built around what's in the van.
