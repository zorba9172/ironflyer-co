A day on board

One typical day.

Days shift with weather and the wind. This is what a calm Wednesday looks like, anchored off Panarea.

06:45
Coffee on deck

Boiled in a moka pot. Quiet, before anything else.

07:15
Morning breathwork

45 minutes of pranayama and circular breath, sunrise at our back.

09:00
Slow breakfast

Local bread, fresh ricotta, tomatoes. The longest meal of the day.

11:00
Sail to the next cove

Three to five hours under sail. Help on the lines if you'd like.

16:00
Anchor, swim, nap

The most important hour. The crew prepares dinner; you do whatever you do.

18:30
Evening session

A short integration breath or a long quiet walk, depending on the day.

20:30
Dinner

Wine on tables we set on shore when the cove allows. Otherwise on deck.

What's included

Everything except the flight.

Berth, board, breathwork, and the boat. We pick you up at Lipari port on Saturday at noon.

Berth in a shared cabin

Two berths per cabin, separate beds. Private cabin upgrade available.

All meals on board

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks. Wine with dinner. Aperitivo most evenings.

Twice-daily breathwork

Sunrise and evening, weather permitting. Adapted on rough days.

One on-shore lunch

Mid-week at a family-run trattoria in Panarea. A long, slow afternoon.

Sailing instruction

As much or as little as you want. The captain enjoys teaching.

Snorkel, paddle, and dinghy

Daily use included. We anchor where the water is worth it.
