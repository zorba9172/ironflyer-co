The atelier

One studio. Two kilns. Three potters.

Lumière began in 2017 as one person and a small electric kiln. Today we are three potters and a wax-pourer, working from a converted tannery in the Vieille Charité.

Every piece is made by a hand we know. Every scent is composed by a perfumer we have worked with for six years.

"We do not make objects for the showroom. We make objects for the kitchen counter, the bedside table, the chair you read in. That is the only test." — Hélène, founder
Inside the boxes

Composed for the senses.

Top notes

Bergamot, salt, fig leaf

The opening minute of every candle. Crisp, citric, alive.

Heart notes

Cypress, neroli, rosemary

The body of the burn. Where Provence lives, ten meters from the kitchen.

Base notes

Smoke, oud, dry stone

What stays on the room after the flame is out, sometimes through the night.
