// Process

How a color appointment actually goes.

Every appointment starts with a fifteen-minute consult in the chair before any product goes on. We'll show you the formula and quote final price before we mix.

01

Consult, in the chair

Show us your reference photos. We'll talk through what your hair can do today and what it'll take over time.

02

Formula on paper

You see the formula on a card. Final price quoted before we mix anything.

03

Color, slow

We don't rush. Most color appointments are two to four hours. Cold seltzer, slow music, no upsell.

04

Bond + send-off

Bond-building treatment, a gloss, and a one-page home-routine card so it lasts to your next visit.
