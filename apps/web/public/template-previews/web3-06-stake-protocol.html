How it works

Three contracts. One token.

Deposit ETH, receive stkETH, redeem any time. Under the hood, your ETH is staked into one of nine distributed node operators via the deposit contract.

01 / deposit

Deposit ETH

Send ETH to the staking pool. You receive stkETH 1:1, instantly. The pool batches deposits and submits to the beacon deposit contract in 32-ETH chunks.

02 / validate

Validators earn

The pool's stake is distributed across nine audited operators. Rewards accrue per epoch and rebase into stkETH on every oracle update — typically every 24 hours.

03 / redeem

Redeem any time

Burn stkETH for ETH via the withdrawal queue, or trade out on Curve, Uniswap, and Balancer for instant exit. The protocol does not lock your liquidity.

Features

Built for everyday stakers.

No hardware. No 32-ETH minimum. No black-box pool operator. Validator set is open, audited, and reported in real time.

∞

No minimum

Stake any amount, from 0.01 ETH up. The pool batches deposits into 32-ETH validator units, so you don't need to.

⌗

9 node operators

Stake is split across nine professional validators across three continents and two client implementations to minimize correlated risk.

⚐

DVT-ready

Distributed Validator Technology integration via Obol and SSV. No single key holds the validator signing power.

$

Composable stkETH

stkETH is an ERC-20 with standard hooks. Use it as collateral on Aave, Morpho, and Spark, or pair it on Curve and Balancer.

↻

Restaking ready

One-click EigenLayer restaking into the operator set of your choice, with a published slashing risk per AVS.

⛬

Open oracle

The reward oracle is a 5-of-9 quorum across the same operators. Every report and every rebase is on-chain.
