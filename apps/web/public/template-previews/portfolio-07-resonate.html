04 — Process

How a commission goes.

Four steps, two cities, one recording day. I quote per-minute of finished music.

I.

Spotting

I watch the cut alone, twice. Then with you, with timecode, and a notebook.

II.

Sketches

Three musical sketches, fully mocked, within ten days. We pick one direction.

III.

Tracking

One or two days at Funkhaus B in Berlin. Live ensemble, single room, no click on long takes.

IV.

Mix & deliver

Final mix in stereo + 5.1, stems, and a session you can revisit ten years later.
