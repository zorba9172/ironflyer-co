Dining

The kitchen that came over from Bombay.

Our head chef Anjali Karnik runs three rooms on the island. All meals are included in the all-inclusive rates and all of them happen with sand under your feet.

  • The pavilion

    Breakfast and dinner under the thatched roof, lit by sixty lanterns after dark.

    Every day · 7 a.m. → 10 p.m.

  • The reef bar
