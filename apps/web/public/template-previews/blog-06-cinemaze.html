About Cinemaze

"We watch the film twice. The second time is the review."

Cinemaze is an independent film publication. We pay our critics. We do not run paid placements, do not accept junkets, and do not write about a film unless we have seen it twice — at least one of those times in a cinema.

Issue 87 was edited in a small office on the Rue de la Pompe, in Paris, and a flat above a print shop in Mexico City. Both of them have very good coffee.
