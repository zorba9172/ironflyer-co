How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Tally is opinionated on purpose. You pick a few habits that matter, you mark the days you keep them, and you reflect once a week.

01

Pick three habits

Tally caps you at three to start. Less feels like less, but more days kept beats a long abandoned list.

02

Mark the days you kept

One tap from the widget, the notification, or the app. Skip days you missed. The streak math is forgiving.

03

Reflect on Sunday

Three prompts arrive Sunday morning. Type a sentence, tap done. Your private log builds week by week.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Tally free?

The core tracker, widget, and Sunday review are free forever. Tally Plus unlocks unlimited habits, an export, and iCloud sync for $2.99 a month or $19 a year.

Does Tally sync between my phone and tablet?

Yes, with Tally Plus. Sync runs through iCloud or your Google account, end-to-end encrypted. Without Plus, your data stays on-device.

What happens if I miss a day?

Nothing dramatic. Tally counts the days you kept inside a rolling window, so a missed day is a small dip, not a reset.

Will you ever add a social feed or leaderboard?

No. Tally is built to be a personal tool. Sharing, comparing, and competing belong somewhere else.

Which platforms do you support?

iOS 16 and later, iPadOS 16 and later, Android 12 and later. A small web reader is in beta for Plus subscribers.
