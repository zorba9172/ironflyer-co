Lisbon, after midnight.

A long week in the old port quarter with six bands who do not yet have managers, a record store that doubles as a bar, and a producer who keeps the keys to two basements you cannot find on a map.

This is the second instalment of our scene reports, which run twice a year. The first, in March, was Bogotá. The next, in October, will be Tbilisi.

Mondays. One mixtape. One letter.

Every Monday morning at 8am: a 45-minute mixtape from the Soundboard staff, one short letter, and the week's two best reviews.

Independent music journalism, paid for.

Soundboard is an independent music publication, made in Lisbon and Berlin, paid for by readers. We do not run sponsored reviews. We do not accept comp'd festival passes from labels we cover. We pay every writer, every photographer, and every translator in this issue.
