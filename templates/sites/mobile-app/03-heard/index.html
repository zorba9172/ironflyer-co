FAQ

Reasonable questions.

How much does Heard cost?

The player, the shortlist, and basic transcripts are free. Heard Plus, $4 a month, adds full back-catalog search, quote clips, and unlimited offline. No ads either way.

Whose transcripts are these?

Most are generated on-device from the audio. Where a publisher provides an official transcript, we prefer that one and credit them. Quotes link back to the show's page.

Will the algorithm push me content?

No. Heard does not have a personalized feed. The home page is the same five shows for everyone this week.

What about chapter skipping?

Hosts who mark chapters get respected. For shows that don't, Heard detects topic shifts and labels them as auto-chapters, clearly marked.

Does Heard work with my CarPlay?

Yes. CarPlay and Android Auto are both supported with a stripped-down interface that's safe to glance at.

Listen better. Read along.

Heard is free to install. Free to keep. Plus is optional and we'll never paywall the core player.
