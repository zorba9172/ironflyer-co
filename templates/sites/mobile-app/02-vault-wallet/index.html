Why Vault

Security with no theater.

We don't ship features that look secure on a marketing page and aren't. Every line below is something a security engineer can verify in the public audits.

[]

Secure Enclave keys

Your private keys live in the device Secure Enclave or StrongBox keystore. Vault servers never touch them, ever.

⌗

Open-source signing

Signing code is open under Apache 2.0 and reproducibly built. Verify the binary on the store matches the commit hash.

≡

Hardware wallet support

Pair with Ledger Nano X, Trezor Safe 3, or Keystone over USB-C or Bluetooth. Multi-sig flows ship in v4.

$

Transparent fees

Vault charges nothing on transfers. Swaps route to public DEXes with the spread shown on the confirmation screen.

○

Phishing shield

Live signature simulator flags drain patterns, unlimited approvals, and known scam contracts before you sign.

⎈

Real recovery

BIP-39 seed, optional Shamir secret-sharing, optional iCloud / Drive encrypted backup. You pick the trade-off.

How it works

From install to first signed transaction.

Three steps, three minutes. No KYC, no email, no analytics pixel watching you set up.

STEP 01

Generate or import

Fresh BIP-39 seed generated on-device, or import an existing wallet. Keys never leave the Secure Enclave.

STEP 02

Back up your way

Write down the seed, split it via Shamir, or encrypt a copy to iCloud. We don't pick for you.

STEP 03

Send, receive, swap

One unified address book across eleven chains. Approvals, simulations, and gas estimates before every signature.

FAQ

What you should ask before trusting a wallet.

Is Vault truly self-custody?

Yes. Your seed is generated on-device, stored in the Secure Enclave / StrongBox, and never transmitted. Vault has no recovery path on its own servers — losing your seed without a backup means losing the wallet.

Where are the audits?

Trail of Bits audited the v3 signer in March 2026. Cure53 audited the bridging path in October 2025. Both reports, with our remediation, are linked from the GitHub repo.

Do you collect analytics?

No third-party SDKs, no IDFA, no advertising ID. Crash reports are opt-in and stripped of wallet metadata.

How do swaps work?

Vault aggregates Uniswap, Curve, Jupiter, and Aerodrome routes. The route, spread, and slippage are shown before signing. Vault takes 0% — the only fees are network gas and the DEX's own spread.

What if I lose my phone?

Restore on a new device from your seed phrase, Shamir shares, or the encrypted iCloud / Drive backup. The choice is yours at setup.
