FAQ

Parents ask.

What does Lumi cost?

Lumi is $6 a month or $48 a year, family-wide (up to 4 kids on one subscription). No ads, no in-app purchases for kids. The first two weeks are free.

What age is this for?

Roughly ages 3 to 7. The path adapts — a curious 3-year-old and a 6-year-old who reads will get very different lessons.

Why only one lesson a day?

Because that's enough. Early-childhood research is clear that short, daily, focused practice beats long sessions. We refuse to gamify more screen time.

Is this COPPA-compliant?

Yes. We're kidSAFE+ COPPA-certified and audited annually. No ads, no third-party tracking, no behavioural profiling. Parent guide has the full policy.

What about offline?

The whole curriculum caches on first login. Lumi works on a plane, in a car, on a cabin weekend without service.

Six good minutes. Every day.

The first two weeks are free. Cancel any time. The screen-time cap stays.
