Why Loop

A sleep app that actually helps you sleep.

Other trackers wake you up with a score. Loop is built around what actually matters in the morning — feeling rested, remembering the dream, and not staring at numbers.

○

Soft wake

Loop wakes you during a light phase inside a 25-minute window, with a sound that fades in from silence over four minutes.

♥

Heart-rate based

Loop reads heart rate and motion from Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, and the Pixel Watch. No phone-on-mattress nonsense.

∮

Dream journal

One swipe in the morning opens a dream journal that auto-tags themes over time. Audio entries supported.

☾

Wind-down stories

Twenty original audio stories, written for sleep, narrated softly. No background music, no music-bed swell. Just voice.

⚌

No score

Loop refuses to grade your night. Instead it shows a trend across two weeks and asks you, gently, how you felt.

⏚

Private

Everything stays on-device unless you opt into encrypted cloud backup. No analytics, no ads, no marketing emails.

FAQ

Quietly, questions.

Do I need a wearable?

No. Loop works watch-free using the phone microphone and accelerometer. A watch adds heart rate and improves phase detection, but it isn't required.

What does Loop cost?

Free, with two wind-down stories included. Loop Plus is $3 a month or $24 a year and unlocks the full story library, multi-week trends, and encrypted backup.

Will Loop wake me up if I'm in deep sleep?

Loop will hold the alarm until you enter a light phase, up to the latest time you set. If you stay deep right to the edge, Loop wakes you anyway — softly.

Is dream-journal data private?

Yes. Entries are stored encrypted on-device. The optional cloud backup is end-to-end encrypted with a key only you hold.

Will Loop replace my therapist or doctor?

No. Loop is a wellness tool. For ongoing insomnia, sleep apnea, or anything that interferes with daily life, please talk to a clinician.

Rest more. Score less.

Loop is free. Plus is optional. The night is yours either way.
