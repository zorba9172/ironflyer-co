FAQ

Sensible questions.

Do I need a hub?

If you have a HomePod mini, Apple TV 4K, Nest Hub, or Echo Hub already, GreenLight will use it as a Thread border router. Otherwise we sell a $39 dedicated dongle. It's optional for Wi-Fi-only setups.

What happens if the internet drops?

Everything inside the home keeps working — scenes, automations, schedules, Siri / Google routines. Remote access is the only thing that pauses.

Does GreenLight have ads?

No. Free tier with up to 25 devices, $4 a month for unlimited devices plus advanced automations. That's it.

How does it work with Apple Home / Google Home?

Two-way. Devices commissioned in GreenLight show up in both. Devices commissioned in Apple Home or Google Home show up in GreenLight.

Where is my data?

Almost all of it stays on your network. The encrypted backup of your scenes and rooms lives in our EU-region servers, and you can disable it.
