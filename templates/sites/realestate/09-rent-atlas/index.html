The rental rules Atlas enforces.

Most rental sites are spam pipelines. Atlas is a closed system: every landlord is verified, every listing is checked, every photo was taken this month.

✓

Verified landlords only

Government ID, deed or lease authority, and a phone number that connects to a person. Or no listing.

⌖

Photos from this month

Every listing photo is timestamped. If a unit re-lists, it gets re-photographed. No stock images, ever.

$

Price-lock on tour

The advertised rent is the rent. If a landlord raises price between tour and lease, Atlas pays the delta for year one.

✕

No application fees

We banned them in 2023. Apply to as many Atlas listings as you want, free.

!

Bait-listing penalty

One bait listing = lifetime ban for the landlord across all 28 markets. We log them publicly.

⊞

Tenant-side guarantee

If your unit is unlivable on move-in day, Atlas pays your hotel and finds you a replacement within 48 hours.
