How it works

Three steps to a study plan.

1

Tell us what you're studying

Pick an exam or upload a syllabus. We map it to vetted decks and timeline blocks within ten seconds.

2

Take a calibration quiz

A 15-minute quiz seeds your starting interval per topic. The app already knows what you know on day one.

3

20 minutes a day

Every morning the app builds a session. Phone, tablet, or web. Twenty minutes is enough on a busy day; do more on a slow one.

FAQ

Things students ask.

How does spaced repetition actually work?

Studyloop schedules each card at increasing intervals based on your last response. The interval grows when you remember and shrinks when you don't, so you spend time on the cards that need it.

Can I import my own deck or notes?

Yes. Pro users can import from Anki, Quizlet, Notion, and a CSV file. We also generate cards from a syllabus PDF in about 30 seconds.

What devices does it run on?

iOS 16+, Android 11+, and any modern browser. The Apple Watch app shows your daily session and streak.

What's the refund policy?

Cancel inside 30 days of any paid plan and you get a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you sell student data?

No. Studyloop is paid by users. We don't run ads, sell data, or share study analytics with third parties.
