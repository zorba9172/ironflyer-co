In the Kitchen

Elena Foschini

Elena grew up in Reggio Emilia, in a kitchen where Sunday started at four in the morning. She trained at Hosteria Giusti in Modena, ran the pasta bench at Locanda Locatelli in London, and opened Vita in 2014 with her brother Marco on a single hand-shake and a copy of her grandmother's recipe book.

The kitchen is small. We make everything we put in front of you — bread, pasta, salumi, gelato, the limoncello. The menu is short because every dish has to earn its place. If we can't do it right, we don't put it on the page.

We are not chasing stars. We are running a neighborhood trattoria the way our grandparents ran theirs.

Elena & Marco FoschiniChef · Owner
