FAQ

Pricing questions.

What counts as a resolved thread?

A thread is resolved when it's marked closed and stays closed for seven days. Reopens within seven days don't double-count.

Do you charge for unresolved threads?

No. Bring as much volume as you want — routing, drafting, and reporting are included. We only charge on the close.

Are reads of the email body stored?

No. We summarize on read and store the summary only. Raw bodies stay with your mailbox provider.

How do you handle PII?

Summaries are redacted for emails, phone numbers, and card numbers before they're stored. The full policy is in the trust center.

Can I bring my own model key?

Yes. Team and Scale customers can connect Anthropic, OpenAI, or self-hosted endpoints for drafts. We charge the platform fee; the provider invoices you.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual contracts save two months on Team and Scale. Enterprise is priced separately.

What happens if I cancel?

You can export your routing rules, your scoring data, and your audit log. We delete everything within 30 days of confirmed cancellation.

Do you have a non-profit discount?

Yes — 40% off Team for verified 501(c)(3) and equivalent organisations.
