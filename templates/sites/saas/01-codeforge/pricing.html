FAQ

Pricing questions, answered.

How is provider cost calculated?

Every paid model call goes through the BillingGuard. Cost lands in the ledger at the rate published by the provider on the day of the call, with the token count signed by the response. You see the same number we do.

What happens if I exceed my plan?

CodeForge warns you at 80% and rate-limits at 100%. We never silently drop runs or hide the bill. You can upgrade in two clicks from the ledger view.

Can I bring my own provider keys?

Yes. Builder, Team, and Enterprise customers can connect Anthropic, OpenAI, or OpenRouter keys directly. We charge the platform fee; the provider invoices you.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual billing saves two months on Builder and Team. Enterprise contracts are priced separately and include a published support SLA.

What does "owner check" mean on Team?

Workspaces, projects, and patches all carry an owner. Only the owner or invited collaborators can read or modify them. Audit log entries record every cross-owner action.

Can I self-host CodeForge?

Enterprise customers can deploy the orchestrator and runtime into their own Kubernetes cluster. We ship a Helm chart and a runbook. Patches still flow through the same gate engine.

How do refunds work?

If you cancel within 30 days of a new Builder or Team subscription, we refund the unused portion in full. Enterprise refunds follow the terms in your signed agreement.

Do you offer a discount for open-source?

Yes. Maintainers of public OSS projects with 1,000+ stars get Builder free. Send us the repo link from contact and we'll set it up.
