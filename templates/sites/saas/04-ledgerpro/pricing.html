FAQ

Pricing questions.

Is journal volume capped?

No. Every plan includes unlimited journal entries. We price on entities, currencies, and standards — not on the number of transactions you book.

What does "policy-as-code" mean?

Revenue recognition and other accounting rules live in a versioned policy file in your tenant. Every booking quotes the rule and the commit. Diffs require approval from a designated reviewer.

Do you support GAAP and IFRS?

Yes. Scale and above include both reporting packs. Switching between standards or running parallel reporting is supported.

How is the audit packet delivered?

One click in the product. The packet includes the trial balance, journal export, supporting documents, and the recognition policy at year-end. Auditors get read-only access on request.

Can I migrate from QuickBooks or Xero?

Yes. Migration is included for Scale and above. Most software companies move in two to four weeks.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual contracts save two months on Scale and Group. Enterprise is priced separately.

Do you support self-hosting?

Enterprise customers can deploy LedgerPro into a VPC or on-prem. We ship a Helm chart and a quarterly upgrade procedure.

How do refunds work?

Cancel within 30 days of a new subscription and we refund the unused portion in full. Enterprise refunds follow the signed contract.
