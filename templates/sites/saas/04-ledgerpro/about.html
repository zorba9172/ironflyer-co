About LedgerPro

Founded by controllers, built for builders.

LedgerPro began the day our co-founder closed her tenth quarterly audit in a spreadsheet. The next morning she sketched a real ledger on a notebook page. Two years later we audit Series C software companies on the back of it.

Origin

Why this company exists.

The accountant's frustration is simple: by the time the books are right, the question has moved on. Revenue recognition rules sit in PDFs, deferred balances are reconciled by hand, and the audit packet is a folder of CSVs that took six weeks to assemble.

We built LedgerPro on three contracts. Policy lives in versioned code, not in PDFs. Every booking quotes the rule and the commit it came from. The audit packet exports itself.

The result is not a faster spreadsheet. It is a real-time double-entry general ledger, with policy-as-code recognition and an export your auditor opens first. The thing we wanted to use, finally available to buy.
