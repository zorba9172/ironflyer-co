// faq

Pricing questions.

does routecloud cost on top of my cloud bill?

Yes — and only for the planner. Your cloud bill stays with your cloud provider. We charge a flat fee for parsing, planning, costing, applying, and rolling back.

what happens when i exceed services on solo?

We warn and rate-limit. We never charge solo users without an upgrade you confirmed.

can i export the terraform?

Always. Every plan compiles to versioned HCL stored in your tenant. You can "break glass" and read the HCL at any time.

which clouds are supported?

AWS, GCP, Fly, and Cloudflare today. Azure is on the roadmap.

is there an annual discount?

Annual contracts save two months on Team and Scale. Enterprise is priced separately.

can i self-host?

Enterprise customers run the planner inside their own kubernetes cluster. We ship a Helm chart and a quarterly upgrade procedure.

how do refunds work?

Cancel within 30 days of a new Team or Scale subscription and we refund the unused portion. Enterprise refunds follow the signed contract.

do you offer an open-source discount?

Yes. Public OSS projects with 1,000+ stars get Team free. Send us the repo link from contact.
