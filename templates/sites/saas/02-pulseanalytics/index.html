The pulse

Analytics with a P&L in mind.

Most product analytics tools show you charts. Pulse shows you the line item the chart created. We connect events, sessions, and orders to ledger entries so every insight has a price tag.

01

Warehouse-native

We read Snowflake, BigQuery, Postgres, and Redshift directly. No copies, no SDKs to fight. The data stays where your warehouse engineers put it.

02

Audit-grade attribution

First-touch, last-touch, multi-touch, position-based — every model is reproducible and footnoted. Auditors get the same number twice.

03

Live revenue cohorts

Cohorts rebuild on stream. A new charge in Stripe updates the LTV curve in under twelve seconds, with the cost basis already netted out.

04

Funnels that explain themselves

Every drop has an annotated cause: deploy, experiment, pricing change, feature flag. Pulse cross-references your change log automatically.

05

Bayesian models with confidence intervals, scenario toggles, and a copy-paste board template. No more spreadsheet roulette.

06

Alerts with verdicts

When a metric breaks, Pulse posts the suspected cause, the dollar impact, and the rollback action — not a red square that means nothing.
