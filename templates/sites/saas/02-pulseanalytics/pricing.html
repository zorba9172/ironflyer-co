FAQ

Pricing questions.

How do you count an event?

One row read from your warehouse in a query that hits a defined event table. Re-running the same dashboard does not re-charge — we cache the read by query fingerprint and re-validate when the upstream changes.

What happens if I exceed my event cap?

We warn at 80% and rate-limit at 100%. Your dashboards continue to serve cached data; new queries queue until you upgrade or the month rolls. We never invoice you for overage without prior consent.

Do you charge per seat?

No. Every plan includes unlimited viewers and editors. Pricing is on event volume.

Can finance get a read-only seat?

Yes. Finance, board, and exec seats are free on every plan, including Starter.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual contracts save two months on Growth and Scale. Enterprise contracts are priced separately.

Do you support on-prem deployment?

Enterprise customers can deploy Pulse inside a VPC or on-prem. We ship a Helm chart, a runbook, and a quarterly upgrade procedure.

How do refunds work?

Cancel within 30 days of a new subscription and we refund the unused portion in full. Enterprise refunds follow the terms in your signed contract.

Will Pulse write back to my warehouse?

No. Pulse is strictly read-only. Connection credentials use a least-privilege role you create.
