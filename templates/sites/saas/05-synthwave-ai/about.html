About Synthwave AI

Trained on your code, not the public internet.

We built Synthwave because the public copilots kept inventing APIs that didn't exist in our codebase. We wanted suggestions that read our real files, followed our real imports, and respected our real conventions. So we built that.

Origin

Why this company exists.

We were three senior engineers on a small team. The public copilots were fast — and often wrong about us. They suggested methods we'd already deprecated, error patterns we never used, and imports from packages we'd left for a fork. The suggestions felt confident. They merged less than half the time.

The fix was obvious in retrospect: index the actual repo. Read the actual files. Cite the actual source. The first prototype was a Friday evening. The first acceptance rate that made us put the laptops down was a Tuesday a month later.

Synthwave is built on a simple promise. Every suggestion cites the file it learned from. Your code never trains a public model. The index runs on your hardware or our SOC 2 cloud — you choose.
