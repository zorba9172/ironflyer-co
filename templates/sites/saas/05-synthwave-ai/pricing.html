FAQ

Pricing questions.

Does my code leave my tenant?

No. Synthwave indexes inside your tenant (Solo and Team) or your own infrastructure (Scale and Enterprise). The DPA contractually prohibits training public models on your code.

Can I bring my own model key?

Yes. Team and above can connect Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, or self-hosted endpoints. We charge the platform fee; the provider invoices you.

What does "cited" mean?

Every suggestion includes one or more files from your repo as its source. Click the citation to jump to the line in your editor.

Which editors are supported?

VS Code, JetBrains, Zed, and Neovim today. Visual Studio and Xcode are on the roadmap.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual contracts save two months on Team and Scale. Enterprise is priced separately.

What happens if I exceed fair-use on Solo?

We warn you and rate-limit. We never charge you on Solo without an upgrade you approved.

Can I self-host the model too?

Enterprise can run Synthwave end-to-end on their own hardware, including the model. We provide the runtime and the runbook.

How do refunds work?

Cancel within 30 days of a new Team or Scale subscription and we refund the unused portion in full.
