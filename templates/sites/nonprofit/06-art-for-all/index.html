Every kid is an artist. Every school deserves an art room.

Art For All puts certified art teachers, real materials, and finished public exhibitions into K-8 schools that lost their art rooms to budget cuts. We pay teacher salaries, ship supplies, and rent the gallery for the year-end show.

What we do

One model, three programs.

Core

Funded Art Rooms

We pay a certified art teacher's salary, ship a full year of supplies, and equip the room. The teacher reports to the school principal but we cover the cost.

$48,400 / school / year

After school

Teaching Artist Residencies

Local working artists run 12-week after-school residencies. Each cohort produces a publicly installed mural, sculpture, or zine. Kids get paid as junior collaborators.

$12,800 / residency

Showcase

Spring Show

Every Art For All school gets a year-end public exhibition. We rent the gallery, frame the work, cater the opening, and bus the families. Real artists, real openings.

$3,200 / show
