From the cooperatives

What the last three months looked like.

A short list from our monthly field digest. Full digests are open in the partner reports section.

March 2026 · Atlantic Rainforest

Nursery #14 hits 220k sapling capacity

The Pernambuco cooperative doubled its nursery throughput this quarter, on track to supply 1.2M saplings for the 2027 planting season.

February 2026 · East African Drylands

Year-5 audit closes at 81% survival in Kitui

An independent audit of our 2020 Kitui plantings closed at 81% live trees — the highest verified year-5 number we've recorded in any biome.

January 2026 · Peat Swamp

Kalimantan crews begin 2026 planting

Our Kalimantan cooperative began the 2026 wet-season planting on January 14, with 800k saplings staged across 12 staging boats.
