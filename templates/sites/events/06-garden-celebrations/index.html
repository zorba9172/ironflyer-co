The studio

We started Garden after a seventieth.

Lena Mosseri opened Garden in 2019 after planning her father's seventieth and realizing how badly the "events industry" served a Sunday lunch for forty people in a backyard. We are now a team of four, and the soft kind of thorough.

We meet you at home, we eat with you, we draw with paper. We have an Excel sheet but we do not show it until the third meeting. The first two meetings are about what the day is actually for.

— Lena, founder

How we work

A planning year, paced gently.

A representative arc for a milestone birthday or bat/bar mitzvah, six to nine months ahead.

01.

Slow start

Two long conversations. We learn the family, the regulars, the people you have to sit far apart.

02.

Direction

A printed direction book — palette, paper, food story, music, lighting. We sit at your kitchen table and walk through it.

03.

Sourcing

Venue, caterer, florist, music. All of them visited in person, all of them references from us.

04.

The day

We arrive early. We hold the timeline. We make the speeches happen on time. You are inside the day, not running it.
