From Distance, in Three Lights Cape Reinga · 04:42 · 2024

Photographs of what is patient enough to be seen.

I work with available light, long lenses, and a small amount of patience. Most of what's here was made between three and six in the morning, somewhere I had to walk to.

02 · Bio

A short paragraph and a working address

b. 1989 · Auckland

I was born in Auckland and trained in photography at the Whitecliffe College of Arts. I've spent the last decade working in long-form documentary, mostly outside cities. The work has been shown at the Trail Foundation, the Northbeam Gallery in Brussels, and the Field & Format pavilion at the 2024 Biennale.

I work slowly on purpose. A series will usually take me eighteen months to call finished, and I print everything myself, in a small darkroom in Devonport. Books are made with Studio Trail and Hangar 12 Press; commissions are taken once a year and only for work I can shoot on film.

If you'd like to see a portfolio in person, I keep working prints in the studio and can usually arrange a visit by appointment.
