How I work, in four steps

Engagements are four to twelve weeks. I price by the project and write a tight scope before we start.

// process/v3
01 / Listen

Week one

I sit in two of your standups and shadow a customer call. Most of the work is here.

02 / Frame

Week two

I send a one-page brief: scope, screens, success metric, and what's deliberately out.

03 / Build

Weeks 3–6

Daily Loom updates. Two weekly syncs. Working file is open the whole time.

04 / Hand off

Final week

Specs, tokens, Storybook stories, and a thirty-minute walkthrough recorded for the team.
