How a commission goes

I keep it simple. Four steps, three weeks usually, one round of revisions built in.

01

You write

A short email with the brief, the deadline, the budget, and where it will run. I'll reply in a day.

02

I sketch

Three pencil thumbnails on tracing paper, photographed on the desk. You pick one.

03

We refine

One round of revisions on the chosen direction. Anything beyond that is billed separately and rarely needed.

04

You receive

Final art in CMYK and RGB, layered, plus the working files. I keep the original on the wall for a week.
