Eighteen days on the Cassin.

A four-person alpine team attempts a winter ascent of Denali's Cassin Ridge. The forecast does not cooperate, the radio goes out on day six, and the youngest climber on the team has to lead the crux. A long account of decisions made in cold places.

About the magazine

We send writers out, and we wait for them to come back.

Wired & Wild is an independent outdoor magazine. We do not run sponsored gear reviews. We do not accept comp'd expeditions. Every writer in this issue went under the magazine's commission, paid the bill, came back, and told us what actually happened — including the parts they would rather not have written down.

If a piece is not finished, we hold it. The mountain is patient. So are we.
