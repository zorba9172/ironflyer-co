From the editor

A letter, instead of a mission statement.

The Tinder began as a Tuesday-night habit. One essay a week, mailed to fourteen people, all of them friends, none of them sponsors. Twelve years later the list is longer and the habit is the same: read deeply, write slowly, ship one essay every Wednesday at six in the morning, and never sell the inbox.

If you find a piece worth your time, forward it to one person. That is the entire growth strategy. It has not failed us yet.

— Ezra Yablon, editor
