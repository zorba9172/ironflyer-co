How it works

Booked direct, nothing hidden.

Cabin is owner-managed. We do not stand between you and the host once you book. The fee is one line on the receipt and your owner has a real phone number.

01

Search & filter

2,418 cabins, by lake, by stove, by region. We let you filter on the things people actually book for.

02

Book direct

One service fee of 6%, printed on the booking. The owner receives 94% of the rate before you arrive.

03

Talk to the owner

Phone number, real name, and a Saturday-morning response time. We do not run a chat queue.

04

Stay & review

Reviews are only allowed from guests who paid. Cabin verifies every one before it goes live.
