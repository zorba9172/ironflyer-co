Formulation principles

If we cannot cite it, we do not put it in the bottle.

Every Tonic product begins with a question we have read enough peer-reviewed studies to answer. If the evidence is thin, we wait. If the evidence is strong, we formulate at the dose used in the trial.

  1. 01.

    One active per bottle

    So you know what works for you and what does not. No more guessing at proprietary blends.

  2. 02.

    Trial-grade dose

    We use the dose the strongest study used. Half-doses are marketing.

  3. 03.

    Third-party CoA

    Every batch is tested by an outside lab. The certificate is linked on the product page.

  4. 04.

    Citations on the page

    Every claim has a footnote. If we cannot link a study, we will not make the claim.
