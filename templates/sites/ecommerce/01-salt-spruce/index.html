Made by

One workshop. Fourteen pairs of hands.

We work with a single family-run atelier in Porto. Every overshirt is cut, joined and finished by a person who signs the inside seam.

No seasonal churn. No overproduction. When a piece sells out, we take six weeks and make it again.

Natural fibers

Linen, brushed wool, undyed cotton — no synthetics in the main cloth.

Six-week reorder

We hold no warehouse. Pieces are cut to the wave of demand.

Repair, not replace

Free mending on every garment for the first three years.

Transparent pricing

Every product page lists fabric cost, labour cost, and our margin.
