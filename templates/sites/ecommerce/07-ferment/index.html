Brewed in Oakland

One brewery. No concentrate.

We started Ferment in 2021 in a back room behind a taqueria. Two people, three plastic buckets, an Excel sheet of pH readings. Today we are a six-person crew with a 300L tank — and the same plastic buckets, kept for sentimental reasons.

Every bottle comes from a wild ferment of whole fruit and real roots. Nothing arrives in a drum. Nothing arrives in a paste.
