● The bindery

Four presses, two binders, one back room in Brooklyn.

PaperCut began as a Vandercook press in a 200-square-foot back room. Today the bindery is six people, four presses, two sewing benches, and one very patient cat. Every notebook is bound by a person whose name is on the inside back cover.

  1. 01.

    Print the covers

    Polymer plates, letterpress, four colors on cotton card.

  2. 02.

    Fold the signatures

    16-page signatures, scored and folded by hand, 12 to a book.

  3. 03.

    Sew & glue

    French link stitch on linen thread, then PVA spine, slow drying.

  4. 04.

    Sign the book

    The binder signs the inside cover. If you can read the signature, they did it on purpose.
