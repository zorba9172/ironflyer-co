# 30-Day Profit Proof Plan

Goal:
Prove users will pay for execution and that Profit Guard protects margin.

## Build

- wallet/top-up
- Profit Guard v1
- 3 blueprints
- Vercel preview
- completion report
- admin profit dashboard
- basic support flow

## Target

- 10 paying users
- 50 paid executions
- avg charge per execution > $10
- avg platform cost per execution < 50% of charge
- preview success > 65%
- refund rate < 15%

## Pass Criteria

```text
gross_margin > 35%
and at least 10 users paid
and at least 30 successful previews
```
