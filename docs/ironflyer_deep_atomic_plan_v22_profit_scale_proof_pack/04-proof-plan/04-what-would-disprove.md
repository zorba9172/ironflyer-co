# What Would Disprove The Business

The system is not economically viable if:

- users do not pay before significant compute is burned
- avg platform cost exceeds 60% of revenue
- refund rate stays above 15%
- repeat usage below 20%
- support time per execution too high
- completion/preview success below 60%
- Profit Guard blocks too many executions
- blueprints do not reduce cost
- learning does not reduce repair cost over time
- frontier model costs dominate every workflow
- abuse/free credit usage consumes meaningful cost

## Kill/Correct Decisions

If these happen:
- narrow scope
- reduce free credits
- increase pricing
- force blueprint-only execution
- limit premium reasoning
- stop unprofitable project categories
