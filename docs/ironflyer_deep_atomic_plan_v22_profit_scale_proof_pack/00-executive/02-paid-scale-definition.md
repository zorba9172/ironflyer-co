# Paid Scale Definition

Paid scale means:

```text
Many users can run executions while the platform stays profitable, stable, and recoverable.
```

## Minimum Paid Scale Milestones

### Milestone 1 — Paid Alpha

- 10–50 paying users
- 100+ paid executions
- gross margin > 35%
- preview success > 70%
- refund rate < 10%

### Milestone 2 — Early Scale

- 100–500 paying users
- 2,000+ paid executions
- gross margin > 45%
- preview success > 80%
- completion-ready or partial-ready > 60%
- average support burden declining

### Milestone 3 — Real Scale

- 1,000+ paying users
- 50,000+ executions
- gross margin > 55%
- repeat usage > 35%
- autonomous recovery success > 70%
- cost per completion trending down

### Milestone 4 — Venture-Scale Signal

- $100K+ MRR
- margin > 60%
- repeat usage growing
- failure/repair genome improves outcomes
- enterprise pilots willing to pay
